PHASE 4 PROMPT:

Build the Director interface. Directors see production volume, 
connect rates, and performance trends. They never see dollar 
amounts. Their job is to drive behavior, not manage compensation.

===================================================================
SCREEN 1 — DIRECTOR HOME
===================================================================

Route: /director (default landing for DIRECTOR)
Source: GET /api/my/team-health + production metrics

THE SCOREBOARD (full width, the most prominent element):

  TODAY'S NUMBERS (large, real-time):
    Sales: {n}    Connects: {n}    Connect Rate: {n}%
    
    Connect rate color:
      >= 75%: green
      60-74%: yellow
      < 60%: red
  
  THIS WEEK:
    Sales: {n} vs last week: {n} ({+/-n}%)
    Connects: {n} vs last week: {n}
    
  THIS MONTH:
    Sales: {n} | Target: {n} | Gap: {n}
    Connects: {n} | Target: {n} | Gap: {n}
    Progress bar toward monthly target

MANAGER LEADERBOARD (below scoreboard):
  Ranked by connects (NOT commission):
  
  Rank | Manager | Team Size | Sales | Connects | Rate | Trend
  
  Trend: sparkline for last 7 days connects
  Color coding by connect rate
  Click manager → drill into their team

REP ALERT PANEL (right side / below on mobile):
  Reps needing attention:
    "No sales in 7+ days" (list of rep names)
    "Connect rate < 50% this week" (list)
    "Open chargeback" (list)
  
  Each alert: rep name, manager name, days since last sale
  [Notify Manager] button per alert

===================================================================
SCREEN 2 — TEAM PRODUCTION
===================================================================

Route: /director/production

FILTER BAR:
  Manager filter | Service filter | Date range
  View: [By Manager] [By Rep] [By Service]

BY MANAGER VIEW:
  Table showing each manager's team performance:
  Manager | Team Size | MTD Sales | MTD Connects | Rate | 
  Best Rep | Needs Attention
  
  Expandable → shows each rep under that manager

BY REP VIEW:
  All reps in the organization ranked by connects:
  Rank | Rep | Manager | Role | Sales | Connects | Rate | 
  Last Sale

BY SERVICE VIEW:
  Production breakdown by service type:
  Service | Total Orders | Connects | Rate | 
  Best Performing Manager

NO DOLLAR AMOUNTS ANYWHERE ON THIS SCREEN.
If a dollar amount field accidentally appears, it must be 
blocked at the API level (stripFinancialFields applied 
to all responses for DIRECTOR role).

===================================================================
SCREEN 3 — TRENDS AND ANALYTICS
===================================================================

Route: /director/analytics

PRODUCTION TREND CHART:
  Line chart: Sales vs Connects over last 90 days
  Toggle: Daily | Weekly | Monthly view
  Overlay: Target connect rate line

CONNECT RATE BY MANAGER (bar chart):
  Each manager as a bar
  Target line overlay
  Color coded (green/yellow/red)

SERVICE MIX (pie/donut chart):
  What percentage of sales are each service tier
  Click segment → shows which managers/reps drive that service

DAY OF WEEK ANALYSIS:
  Which days generate most sales
  Which days generate most connects
  Heatmap view

COHORT ANALYSIS (rep performance over time):
  New reps: sales in first 30/60/90 days
  Trend lines for cohort groups
  Identifies training effectiveness

===================================================================
SCREEN 4 — ORDER APPROVALS
===================================================================

Route: /director/approvals

Directors can approve orders. This is their operational 
contribution — keeping the approval pipeline moving.

APPROVAL QUEUE:
  Orders needing approval (all teams visible to DIRECTOR)
  
  Each row:
    Rep | Customer | Service | Date | Days waiting
    Auto-approval result: Clean ✓ or Flagged ⚠ (with reasons)
    NO commission amount shown
  
  [Approve] [Reject] [Skip for now] per row
  [Approve All Clean] batch button

APPROVAL HISTORY:
  Director's own approval history
  Date | Order | Rep | Decision | Time to approve

===================================================================
SCREEN 5 — KNOWLEDGE AND GOALS
===================================================================

Route: /director/resources

KNOWLEDGE DOCUMENTS:
  Read + write access
  Organized by category
  [+ Add Document] button
  Used to share training materials, product updates, 
  field strategies with the team

SALES GOALS:
  Set goals for any manager or rep
  Goal type: sales count | connect rate | specific service
  Period: weekly | monthly
  
  Goal tracking table:
    Rep/Manager | Goal | Current | % Complete | Days Left
  
  [Set New Goal] button → opens goal creation form
