PHASE 5 PROMPT:

Build the Executive interface. Executives are business owners.
Their dashboard answers one question every morning:
"Is the business healthy and growing?"

===================================================================
SCREEN 1 — EXECUTIVE HOME
===================================================================

Route: /executive (default landing for EXECUTIVE)

THE BUSINESS AT A GLANCE (first fold — no scrolling needed):

  Three giant numbers:
  
  ┌─────────────────┐  ┌─────────────────┐  ┌─────────────────┐
  │   MTD REVENUE   │  │  MTD PROFIT     │  │  PROFIT MARGIN  │
  │    $XX,XXX      │  │   $XX,XXX       │  │     XX%         │
  │  +X% vs target  │  │  +X% vs LM      │  │  Target: XX%   │
  └─────────────────┘  └─────────────────┘  └─────────────────┘

  Below the three numbers:
  Production: {connects} connects | {sales} sold | {rate}% rate
  
  Trend arrows on everything (up/down vs last month)

EXCEPTION SUMMARY (second fold):
  ALL exception types (unlike other roles)
  
  Shown as a risk matrix:
  
  CRITICAL (requires Executive decision):
    - Negative margin detected on {n} orders
    - Pay run overdue for finalization
    - Large AR variance: ${X} outstanding from {client}
  
  OPERATIONAL (being handled by Operations):
    - {n} orders pending approval
    - {n} installs to sync
  
  FINANCIAL (being handled by Accounting):
    - {n} overrides pending approval
    - {n} pay stubs in draft

  "No action needed" items are collapsed/greyed out

CASH FLOW SUMMARY:
  Simple two-number comparison:
  Received from carriers this month: $X
  Paid to reps this month: $X
  ─────────────────────────────────
  Net cash position: $X

===================================================================
SCREEN 2 — FULL FINANCIAL VIEW
===================================================================

Route: /executive/financials
Source: GET /api/my/financial-snapshot

PERIOD COMPARISON:
  This Month vs Last Month side by side
  
  Revenue (rack rate total)
  Rep payouts (total commissions paid)
  Override payouts (director + admin + accounting)
  Iron Crest Profit
  Profit Margin %
  
  All with month-over-month delta and direction arrow

PROFIT BY SERVICE (bar chart):
  How much profit each service tier generates
  Shows which services are most profitable
  Colors by margin percentage

PROFIT BY MANAGER (horizontal bar):
  Which managers' teams generate most profit
  Note: this shows profit generated by their team's sales,
  not the manager's personal earnings

PAYROLL OBLIGATIONS PANEL:
  What's committed but not yet paid:
  Ready-to-pay orders: ${X} ({n} orders)
  Override earnings approved: ${X}
  Advances outstanding: ${X}
  Total obligation: ${X}
  
  "Estimated next pay run total: ${X}"

AR HEALTH PANEL:
  Collection rate (last 30 days): {n}%
  Overdue from carriers: ${X}
  Average days to collect: {n}
  Risk: {client name} ${X} overdue {n} days

===================================================================
SCREEN 3 — FULL PRODUCTION VIEW
===================================================================

Route: /executive/production

Identical to Director production view BUT with dollar amounts.

Executive sees everything Director sees plus:
  Commission per connect shown next to connects
  Total payout column added to all tables
  Profit column added to service breakdown

===================================================================
SCREEN 4 — OVERRIDE APPROVALS (Executive Authority)
===================================================================

Route: /executive/overrides

TABS:
  Director Overrides (only EXECUTIVE can approve these)
  Accounting Overrides
  All Pending

Same card format as Accounting override screen but 
Executive has authority over ALL override types.

[Bulk Approve All] — single button to clear the queue

===================================================================
SCREEN 5 — COMPANY SETTINGS
===================================================================

Route: /executive/settings

Full system settings access:
  
  RATE CARDS:
    View and edit all rate cards
    Commission amounts by role
    Effective date management
    [+ Add Rate Card] button
  
  OVERRIDE AGREEMENTS:
    Override amounts by service tier
    Recipient role configuration
  
  AUTOMATION RULES:
    Toggle auto-approval on/off
    Set confidence thresholds
    Configure auto-post settings
  
  PAYROLL SETTINGS:
    Scheduled pay run configuration
    Pay period definitions
  
  USER MANAGEMENT:
    Full user roster
    Only screen where EXECUTIVE/ACCOUNTING users can be created
    Role change capability (with audit log)
  
  SYSTEM HEALTH:
    Background jobs history
    Error log
    Database performance metrics
